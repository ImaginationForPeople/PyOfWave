PyOfWave

About

PyOfWave (POW) aims to bring the Google designed communications platform back in a healthy, grass roots, decentralized fashion. We aim to be extendable, sufficintly fast, flexible, and user freindly.

For the geeks and corperations using POW to offer Wave services we offer flexibility. POW will fit whatever persistance, authentication, and protocol setup you have.

For end user's, we will offer a web interface inspired by the advice published by Edward Tufte and Apple Inc. to offer a superior user experience.
